Problems That We Are Addressing

Problems

Limited access to veterinary services: Only about 25% of Pakistani livestock farmers can access animal health services, leading to increased disease prevalence and reduced livestock productivity.

Lack of formal education and training: Many farmers lack the knowledge and skills necessary for modern farming practices, further inhibiting their ability to effectively manage their livestock and control diseases.

Methane emissions from ruminant animals like cows are a key contributor to climate change. The process, called enteric fermentation, is not only harmful to the environment, but also wastes potential energy resources.

Omnigrow, our startup, tackles this issue by efficiently capturing the methane these animals emit. The captured methane is then converted into biogas, a renewable energy source for heating, electricity, and vehicle fuel. Thus, Omnigrow not only reduces greenhouse gas emissions but also helps farms become energy self-sufficient and unlocks a new, sustainable energy source.

The food supply chain is facing significant issues due to inadequate regulatory systems, particularly in the dairy and meat sectors. One of the most alarming problems is adulteration, where these products are tainted with harmful substances, reducing their quality and posing serious health risks to consumers. The lack of transparency in the supply chain, coupled with weak monitoring systems, allows such malpractices to persist and even thrive in some markets. To address this problem, Omnigrow, our start-up, is committed to leveraging state-of-the-art technologies to strengthen regulatory systems and ensure product integrity. Our platform uses blockchain technology to bring transparency to the food supply chain. By providing an immutable, decentralized ledger of all transactions, we offer a complete traceability solution from farm to fork.

Omnigrow ensures each step of an animal's life and the production of milk and meat products is accurately recorded and verifiable. Consumers can access this information to verify the safety and authenticity of the products they consume. On the other side, producers and retailers benefit from the assurance that their products are free from adulteration, promoting consumer trust and loyalty. By tackling the issues at the root, Omnigrow is set to revolutionize the food supply chain and address the widespread problem of adulteration in milk and meat products.

The informal livestock market, worth nearly $40 billion, faces challenges in animal traceability, health monitoring, and performance recording. This hampers food safety, export potential, and increases disease risks.

Omnigrow aims to revolutionize this sector by leveraging cutting-edge technology for comprehensive animal management. We provide traceability for food safety, health information to prevent diseases, and performance data for optimizing productivity. Additionally, our system facilitates export compliance, helping producers enter international markets. Through our data-driven approach, Omnigrow aims to mitigate losses and boost animal welfare, formalizing this large market.
